Challenge Your Mind

Brain games can help us enhance our reflexes, memory, and logical reasoning.


Play Chess

Improves Concentration

Playing chess can improve cognitive skills like memory, planning, and problem-solving. In a single game of chess, there are 400 possible moves after each move played. It is possible to checkmate your opponent in just two moves.

Try SUDOKU

Skill that does not involve Luck or Fluky Chances

Sudoku is a logic based number placement puzzle. The objective is to fill a 9×9 grid so that each column, each row, and each of the nine 3×3 boxes (also called blocks or regions) contains the digits from 1 to 9, only one time each.

Play Tic-TAc-Toe

Simple to Grasp, Easy to Adapt

Tic-Tac-Toe is well known game that is simple to grasp, easy to adapt and excellent for building beginner planning, problem solving and perspective taking skills. Also as called Noughts and Crosses.
